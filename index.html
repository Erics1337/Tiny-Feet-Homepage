Climate planning in your community is important!

The UNEP and IPCC advise that global emissions need to be cut by over half by 2030 in order to keep global warming below a 5.3C threshold - a critical tipping point that if exceeded will have catastrophic consequences to all planetary systems

Tiny Feet amalgamates the best practices of existing and emerging greenhouse gas emissions modeling with available source emissions data
